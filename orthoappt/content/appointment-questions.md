## About your specific exercises in Pull A and Legs A

**On the SL leg press (right leg) at a 90° hip flexion ceiling.** "I'm doing single-leg leg press on my uninvolved right leg with the foot set high to keep hip flexion at or below 90° because of the FAI on that side.
